Incoterms Cheat Sheet

International shipping regulations defining responsibility maps:

EXW (Ex Works)

Buyer picks up goods from your factory floor. Minimal risk to you.

FOB (Free On Board)

You cover costs until delivery onto the shipping vessel. Common benchmark.

CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight)

You pay freight and insurance to the destination port.
